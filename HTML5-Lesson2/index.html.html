Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here ís a recipe for delicously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Methods

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and scream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
